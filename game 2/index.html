Forsaken

  • Originality: The assets are modified by me based on the assets I have.
  • Technical Merit: Realize the interaction between player and other objects
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learn that there are many way to realize funcitonality, I need to keep my mind open to avoid unnecessary thinking. For example, to setup a new world bound, i can just add limitation to the movements.
  • Prototype Assets: I borrow game assets from free game assets website.
    • Image: Itch.io

  • High Concept: Collect keys in order to open final doors to get back the identification
  • Theme: Lost all identification
  • Mandated Variety: For input to control movements: up, down, left, right. Space to interact with doors.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is focus on the interaction between objects.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should keep thinking in the game experience.
  • Gameplay: To find keys laying in different rooms in the jail in order to open the final door.
  • Strategies: keep moving, pay attention to the upcoming changes.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are somehow put into jail. You forget everything, even who you are, and you lost all identifications. In order to recall your identity, you need to find your records in this strange jail.
  • Target Audience: Players whom like puzzles.
  • Play Time: About a minute to finish a game
