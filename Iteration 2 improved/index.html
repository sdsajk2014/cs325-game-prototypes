  • High Concept: Use your hot weapon to beat the cold monster
  • Theme: Soldier.
  • Mandated Variety: W,A,S,D for movements, J for shotting, space for starting a stage.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is a pratice for me to know how to make a real functional game.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will feel excited to escape from the monsters' chase and use shotgun to beat them.
  • Gameplay: Excape from monsters, and use shotgun to shot with enemies.
  • Strategies: Hit and Run.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The world is becoming "cold" because of the burst of monsters. As a former soldier, you decided to pick up your shotgun, and use your "hot" weapon to fight with the "cold" world.
  • Target Audience: Players whom like shotting games.
  • Play Time: About 10 minute to finish the game.
