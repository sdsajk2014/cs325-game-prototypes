  • High Concept: Try to beat your enemy and find a way to escape from the jail.
  • Theme: Caught.
  • Mandated Variety: Left and right arrow keys to control the direction of movements, space to jump, and A key to fire bullets.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is a pratice for me to know animation of the phaser
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel excited and keep thinking in the game experience.
  • Gameplay: Try to beat your enemy and find a way to escape from the jail.
  • Strategies: Keep distance with enemies, they can touch you from far away.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You were somehow being caught and put into jail, when you wook up, you found that there are many clones of yourself. You feel scared and angry. You put up your weapon, want to kill all of clones and escape the jail.
  • Target Audience: Players whom like puzzles.
  • Play Time: About a minute to finish the game.
