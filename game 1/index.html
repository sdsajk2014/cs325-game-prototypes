Golfer General

  • Originality: The control of the plane is different from other games. You can never stop!
  • Technical Merit: Try to keep the update function simple, which can improve performance
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learnt that the setup of the enviroment is really complicated. The hardest part is to fix bugs in update() function.
  • Prototype Assets: I borrow from the assets from examples in phaser's offical website, and some others from free gameset website.
    • Example: Invaders
    • Website: Aigei.com
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
    • Example: Invaders
  • High Concept: Drive with your plane to destory and escape from coming enemies.
  • Theme: Golfer.
  • Mandated Variety: For input to control movements: up, down, left, right. Space to shoot the bullet.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is focus on the controal of the game.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel concentrate and excited when playing games.
  • Gameplay: Players should try to dodge from coming enemies, use bullets to elimate them and try to get 200 scores to win the game.
  • Strategies: keep moving, pay attention to the upcoming changes.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The driver of the plane was once a outstanding golfer. After war began, he decided to take part in the airfore and defend his hometown.
  • Target Audience: Players like dodge ball would like the game
  • Play Time: About a minute to finish a game
